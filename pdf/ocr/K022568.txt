Description of the ProLite / Plasmalite The ProLite / Plasmalite MPX Pulsed Light System delivers
MPX Pulsed Light System pulsed light at a wavelength beginning at a wavelength of
600 nm. The device consists of three interconnected
sections: The cabinet which houses the internal cooling
system, power supply and microcontroller, the umbilical to
the handpiece, and the handpiece, which houses the
waveguide
Intended use of the ProLite Pulsed Light The ProLite / Plasmalite MPX Pulsed Light System is
System indicated for use to remove unwanted hair in all skin types
according to the Fitzpatrick Scale.
Performance Data: None
Conclusion: The ProLite / Plasmalite MPX Pulsed Light System is
substantially equivalent to other existing pulsed light
systems in commercial distribution for removal of unwanted
hair in all skin types according to the Fitzpatrick Scale.
