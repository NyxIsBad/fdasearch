Device Description:
The Malis 2000 Bipolar Electrosurgical System includes the generator, a
Bipedal footswitch for operating the cutting and coagulation functions, a
connecting cable to allow the use of a MALIS irrigator with the MALIS
2000 generator. The unit incorporates the use of a LCD panel to make it
easier for the surgeon to see the setting.
